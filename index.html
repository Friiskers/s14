Background
Grisha Lyonya grew up on the outskirts of Kyiv, listening to stories of the forbidden Zone whispered by drunk soldiers and half-crazy wanderers. After the war and hardship left his life in ruins, he turned his eyes to the north — to Chernobyl — chasing the rumors of glowing riches, forgotten bunkers, and artifacts that could bend reality. Equipped with a cheap Geiger counter and an aging AKS-74U, he crossed into the Zone not as a soldier or scientist, but as a man with nothing left to lose. Still green and learning the ropes, he’s a Rookie Stalker with fire in his blood and death always one wrong step away.
